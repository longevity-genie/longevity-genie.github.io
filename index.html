Unlike other tales where a genie would grant you wishes, the Longevity Genie would answer questions about ageing research and health data, but with unparalleled accuracy.

Problems arise

On a starry night, during the grand Zuzalu Hackathon, visionary alchemists and sages convened.

They spoke with the mighty Large Language Models about reaching healthy longevity and learned their limitations, as they are:

  • often ensnared by illusions and hallucinations.
  • wandering astray, unable to tap into the sacred wells of longevity (no interactions with academic research papers and structured biomedical databases)
  • blind to the personal scrolls of health (personal health data)

Longevity-Genie gifts

From the fires of Zuzalu hackathon inspired by the ancient legends, the first code of the Longevity Genie was forged, shimmering with promise and echoing the ancient tales.

Its gifts were many:

  • Sifting through the sands of biomedical research, indexing acedemic scrolls with language of AI inside the mystical chambers of Longevity-GPT and retrieving them in the context when they are needed
  • Guided by LLM-powered spirits to commune with biomedical and aging tomes and ReAct by searching aging databases.
  • A gaze that could read one's personal health scrolls, crafting answers tailored to the seeker's essence.

The organisation

As dawn broke, a new beacon appeared on the horizon: the Longevity Genie Organization on GitHub. Within its open-source repositories, the secrets and spells of the Longevity Genie were shared, inviting all to partake in its magic. Behind this grand endeavor, a group of visionaries included:

  • Newton Winter (GitHub): The code sorcerer and creator of the chat bot's emblem.
  • Anton Kulaga (GitHub, LinkedIn, Facebook): The data architect and guiding force behind the team's direction.
  • Alex Karmazin (GitHub, LinkedIn): The LLM whisperer, adept at database management and prompt crafting.
  • Maria Koval (GitHub, LinkedIn): The artistic spirit with LLM whispering powers, shaping the Genage LLM agent and Zuzalu's visuals.
  • Nikhil Yadala (X/Twitter): LongevityGPT creator, merging the might of LongevityGPT with the Longevity Genie.

Together, they embarked on a quest to unveil the magic of the Longevity Genie to the world. Yet, for the legend to flourish, more was needed. The call went out across Zuzalu and beyond: gitcoin funding is required to propel the project forward, to let the Genie's grant three wishes:

  1. To forge a multi-modal, multi-hop Q & A pipeline
  2. To make it free from the mirages of hallucination
  3. To make it rich in the lore of aging-related questions and research.
