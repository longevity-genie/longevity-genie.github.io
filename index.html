What is Longevity-Genie?

Longevity Genie is a conversational bot, which allows users to easily upload their health-related data through API-integration with health apps. Users can also connect external databases while interrogating bot on longevity research related matters. The bot is capable of tracing all scientific statements back to original source articles, takes into consideration user’s health data while giving answers and can be updated on its knowledge of biological concepts through third party databases integration. Therefore Longevity Genie offers an advantage over traditional LLMs in certain tasks, where it is required to apply theoretical knowledge in the context of user’s data.

Problem statement

  • LLMs do not use specialized longevity-related datasets
  • LLMs basically do not have easy access to your health related data and cannot take it into account
